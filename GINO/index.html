A little About Gino

Gino Goss is an Award winning Producer/Artist, Trumpeter, Latin percussionist Gino Goss decided in 2008 to embark on his own, forming Gino Goss and Jammajazz, producing a unique and exciting sound that is all his own. In 2009, he released is debut CD entitled ALMOST FAMOUS to glowing reviews. Various cuts from this freshman CD can be heard globally.
